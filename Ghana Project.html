About Ghana

Ghana is a country located in West Africa. It is known for its rich culture, gold resources, cocoa production, and beautiful beaches. Accra is the capital city.

Ghana was the first African country to gain independence from colonial rule in 1957, and it is known as the "Gateway to Africa."
